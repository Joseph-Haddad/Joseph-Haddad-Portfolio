Crane
Project

This project is on the development of a crane which is designed to hold 1.5kg, move it to a location then stop.

The development of this crane proved to be a good challenge. Working as a team, we needed to combine all of our skills to create the crane. It tackles most aspects of engineering. Electrical, Mechanical and Civil. For me this was an exciting learning opportunity as it was the first project I had ever physically constructed at uni. I learnt not only how to function as a team to create the crane. But to look into the set specifications as oulined by the report. Test materials, and then select what are the best options for this device. Which could translate into real world as good cost saving measures whej applied to larger projects.
